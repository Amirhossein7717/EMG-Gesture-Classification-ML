HOW TO COMPILE THE LATEX REPORT
================================

1. COPY YOUR FIGURES
   Copy these PNG files from where your notebook saved them
   into the  latex_report/figures/  folder:

     visualisation.png
     kruskal_boxplot.png
     pca_scatter.png
     feature_set_comparison.png
     confusion_roc.png
     feature_importance.png     <- new
     gridsearch_heatmap.png     <- new
     crossval.png
     comparison.png

2. COMPILE (run twice so table of contents is correct)
   In the latex_report/ folder:

     pdflatex main.tex
     bibtex main
     pdflatex main.tex
     pdflatex main.tex

   Or open main.tex in Overleaf, upload all files and figures,
   and click Compile.

3. OUTPUT
   main.pdf  -- your final submission report
